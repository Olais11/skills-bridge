Bridging the Gap Between Youth Potential and Employment Opportunities

Mission

To combat youth unemployment in Tanzania by bridging the gap between education and employment through innovative skills development, technology-driven solutions, and strategic partnerships that create sustainable opportunities for Tanzania's youth population.

Vision

A Tanzania where every young person has access to meaningful employment opportunities, where skills development is accessible and relevant, and where youth are empowered to become job creators rather than just job seekers. By 2030, we envision reducing youth unemployment by 50% through strategic interventions in digital literacy, entrepreneurship training, and industry partnerships that align with Tanzania's Development Vision 2025 and the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

Why This Matters

Economic Growth

Reducing youth unemployment contributes directly to Tanzania's economic development and poverty reduction goals.

Social Stability

Employed youth are more likely to contribute positively to their communities and participate in civic activities.

Future Innovation

Skilled youth drive innovation and entrepreneurship, creating new opportunities for future generations.
