Our Mission & Impact

Mission Statement

To combat youth unemployment in Tanzania by bridging the gap between education and employment through innovative skills development, technology-driven solutions, and strategic partnerships that create sustainable opportunities for Tanzania's youth population.

The Challenge

Despite Tanzania's impressive economic growth averaging 7% over the past decade, this growth has not translated into sufficient productive and decent employment opportunities for youth. The main barriers include:

Skills Mismatch

Educational curricula often fail to align with labor market needs, particularly in soft skills like communication, teamwork, and language proficiency. Employers frequently need to retrain new hires.

Limited Access to Training

With 70% of youth aged 14-17 not enrolled in secondary education, many enter the workforce without foundational skills or relevant vocational training.

Digital Skills Gap

Two-thirds of young people cannot access the internet at home, limiting their ability to participate in the digital economy and acquire essential 21st-century skills.

Our Approach

1. Skills Development Programs

Comprehensive vocational training focused on high-demand sectors including technology, agriculture, construction, and creative industries, with emphasis on both technical and soft skills.

2. Industry Partnerships

Collaboration with local businesses and the Association of Tanzanian Employers to create apprenticeships, internships, and direct employment pathways.

3. Entrepreneurship Support

Business training and mentorship programs to help youth start and grow their own enterprises, contributing to job creation beyond individual employment.
